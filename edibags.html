EdiBags
40% of great tasting fruits and vegetables are wasted and thrown away because of cosmetic imperfections
EdiBags reduces both food and plastic waste by forming an edible sandwich bag from cosmetically damaged produce that are otherwise thrown away.

There are currently 75 million school aged children within the United States.

80% of them do not receive their daily recommended amount of fruits and vegetables.

Edibags presents a new way to fill this gap in children's diets.

Enough Plastic is discarded each year to circle the earth 4 times.

Simple steps can be taken to reduce this. One easy idea: get rid of the plastic sandwich bags we use to carry our favorite finger foods and sandwiches with us.
